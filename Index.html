About Steven Sibuyi Attorneys

Steven Sibuyi Attorneys Inc was formed in 2018 and is based in Hazyview, Mpumalanga. Founded by Mr. Steven Sibuyi, the firm provides sound legal advice and quality representation across a wide spectrum of matters. The firm is committed to the pursuit of excellence and the effective finalisation of all matters presented to it.

Vision

To be a dynamic legal firm, providing professional assistance and committing ourselves to the pursuit of excellence.

Mission

To become a reputable law firm in South Africa and beyond through providing reliable, timely, effective and efficient legal services to clients.

Motto

"Our Practice is Your Solution"

Our Services — Full list

We offer the following services with experience and dedication. Click each item for a short explanation.

Civil & Criminal Litigation

Representation in civil disputes and criminal matters — from pleadings and discovery to courtroom advocacy and appeals.

Wills & Estates

Drafting clear and enforceable wills, estate administration, executor services, and advice on succession planning.

Registration of Trusts, Companies & Other Businesses

Formation and registration services for trusts, private companies, and other legal entities; compliance and governance advice.

Tenant Evictions & Rental Claims

Efficient eviction processes and recovery of rental arrears while ensuring compliance with rental housing legislation.

Correspondent Attorney Services

Acting as local correspondent attorneys for other law firms — attending to briefed matters in our jurisdiction and communicating progress promptly.

Family Law & Divorce Matters

Support with divorce proceedings, maintenance claims, contact and guardianship matters, always with sensitivity to family dynamics.

Third Party (RAF) Claims

Assistance with Road Accident Fund claims and third-party compensation matters, including claim assessment and litigation where required.

Police Unlawful Arrests, Assault & Detention

Representation and remedies for unlawful arrest, police assault, wrongful detention and related civil claims against the state.

Medical Malpractice & Negligence Claims

Pursuing claims for medical negligence and malpractice, working with experts to establish breach and causation for compensation.
